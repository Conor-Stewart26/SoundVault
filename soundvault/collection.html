A Note on Collecting

For me, buying a physical record is a commitment. You can't just skip a track — you sit with the whole thing, front to back, the way the artist intended. That discipline has changed how I hear music and honestly how I think about it. Every piece in this collection has a memory attached to it, and that's something a streaming algorithm can't replicate.

I update this catalog as new pieces come in. Check the blog for write-ups on recent additions and the stories behind them.
