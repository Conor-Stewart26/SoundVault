About These Stats

All of the data on this page is derived directly from my physical record collection. Rather than pulling from a streaming platform, these stats reflect what I've actually chosen to own — which to me is a much more honest picture of my musical identity. Every record here was a deliberate purchase, and the patterns that emerge from that tell a more meaningful story than a play count ever could.
